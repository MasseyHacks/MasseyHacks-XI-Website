What is MasseyHacks?
+
MasseyHacks is a high school hackathon perfect for students fascinated by the world of technology. This 24-hour event is an opportunity for students to explore the realm of computer science and bring their creative ideas to life by developing a project of their own. Never written a line of code in your life? Already created a robot to do your homework? No matter your skill level, we invite you to join us as a hacker to participate in workshops, engage in countless activities, and meet other like-minded students! Create memories and guide the story of your STEM journey at MasseyHacks XI!

Q: Does it cost anything to attend?

Nope, MasseyHacks is absolutely free to attend!

Q: Will food be provided?

Yes, meals and snacks will be provided free of cost. We will accommodate any food sensitivities to the best of our ability.

Q: Who can participate?

MasseyHacks welcomes students from grades 7-12.

Q: Do I need programming experience to participate?

Not at all! At MasseyHacks, we'll teach you the fundamentals you need to know to make your project through beginner workshops and mentors who will assist you if you ever need any help.

Q: How many people can I have on my team?

You can have a maximum of 4 people on your team!

Q: Will MasseyHacks be overnight?

Unfortunately, we cannot provide overnight accommodation at the MasseyHacks venue, so hackers will be required to leave the venue Saturday evening and return to the venue Sunday morning. However, we will still be providing mentorship and support for hackers throughout the night as they continue working on their projects.

Q: What do I need to bring?

We recommend bringing the following:

- A piece of photo ID for check-in (e.g. student card, passport, or driver's license)
- Your laptop
- Laptop/phone chargers

Food, beverages, and the rest will all be provided! School computers will be available for use during the event, but we cannot guarantee one for everyone.

Q: What activities and workshops will be at MasseyHacks?

A variety of fun activities like cup-stacking, karaoke, and therapy dogs and a diverse selection of computer science workshops will be available throughout the event. These will be featured on the schedule which will be posted closer to the event date!
